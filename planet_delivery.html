Planet Delivery

This Project started as a way to put into practice the things I learned during my year in Saga School.

Unreal Engine 5 was the engine used and Blueprints were the language used for all the code for the game.

There was 1 more developer working with me, and they made all the 3D assets excluding the player Ship.

While i programed all of the sistems in the game, i want to highlight the Gravity sistem used in the planets and the Player Ship. As both of these sistems were made from scratch to better understand Unreal Engine.

I made most of the textures in the game using a mix of Substance Painter and Substance Designer. The SpaceShip model was made in Blender and the Sounds were mixed in Reaper.
