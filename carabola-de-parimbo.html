Carábola de Parimbo

This Game was made in 11 days by a team of 3 in Godot Engine, where i was responsible for the code, sound design, Game Design, writing and narration

One developer helped with the code for most of the Ui and some logic for the level, i made the textures for the levels and the levels themselves, while the assets were made by another team member.

I made all of the sounds and narration using Reaper and Fmod. Wrote all the dialogue from the narrator and placed the assets, lights, triggers and so on on the levels.
