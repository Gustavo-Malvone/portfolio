Spire

Spire is my Solo Developer Project made in the Godot Engine.

The goal with this project is to showcase my skills and develop a demo to something that could become a comercial project down the line.

The main mechanic is the ability to grab onto any object that has a valid Ledge, this idea was taken from the game White Knuckle and proved to be quite a chalenge to get working. The whole system is physics based and Godot provides a simple framework, so most of the physics were tweaked manually to achieve the desired effect.

All of the sounds were made in Reaper and then integrated into the Engine using Fmod.

While the Demo is still very short with only 2 levels and no Model for the hands or the Player, I believe it still manages to showcase quite a lot.

The Multiplayer system uses Godot own high level Multiplayer Framework combined with a community addon called NodeTunnel to perform the peer to peer connection.
