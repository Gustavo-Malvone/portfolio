Who am I

Hello! My name is Gustavo Malvone I am a Game Developer from Brazil, I was a professional Back-End programmer but now I make games.

While my main area of expertise is coding, I have studied 3D modeling/texturing for characters and environments at Saga, one of the biggest schools for 3D artists in South America, and I designed every sound used in the projects below.

I love to learn new skills and techniques, from the sound design, to the code and even the visual elements of a game. I love getting involved in every aspect of Game Development.
